Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Éclat Peau?
Éclat Peau is a skincare solution designed to help individuals achieve their skin goals through personalized recommendations and guidance.
How does the system work?
The system analyzes uploaded images and provides tailored skincare advice based on individual skin conditions and needs.
Do I need to register to use Éclat Peau?
Yes, registration is required to access personalized features and save your preferences.
Are there any limitations on the type of images that can be uploaded?
We recommend using clear, well-lit images of your face without filters to ensure accurate analysis.
Are the ingredient recommendations universal?
Ingredient recommendations are personalized based on your skin type and conditions, so they may vary between users.
Is the system safe to use?
Yes, the system is designed with user safety in mind, and we prioritize data privacy and security.
What should I do if I’m not satisfied with the recommendations?
If you're not satisfied, we encourage you to reach out to our support team for further assistance and adjustments.
Can Éclat Peau be used for all skin types?
Yes, Éclat Peau is designed to cater to all skin types, providing tailored solutions for each individual.
Where can I find more information?
More information can be found on our website or by contacting our support team.
How can I reach the support team?
You can reach our support team via the contact page on our website or through our social media channels.
